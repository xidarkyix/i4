FAQ

Is this legal and allowed by platforms?

We only proceed where a transfer path complies with applicable laws and the platform’s rules. We refuse hacked, stolen, or fabricated ownership. Some platforms restrict buying/selling usernames—when a swap isn’t policy‑safe, we won’t do it.

What proof do you require?

Photo ID match (KYC), domain/email verification, prior usage evidence, and/or trademark docs depending on the case.

How do payments work?

We can use third‑party escrow or milestone payments. Fees depend on complexity and platform risk.

Compliance Notice: We do not facilitate unauthorized access, impersonation, or policy violations. All services require proof of ownership and legitimate intent. Availability of a transfer is not guaranteed and depends on platform rules.
